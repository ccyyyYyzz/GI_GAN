| Phase range | Role | Current use |

## Figure-System History

The figure-history phases are useful for reconstructing design decisions:

- Phase38-44: professional figure, signal trace, closed-loop figure, operator circuit, operator centering.
- Phase50: final Figure 1 assembly attempt and table/figure synchronization.

Safe use:

- Reuse design concepts, layout ideas, and figure assets after checking they match the canonical story.

Do not use:

- Old figure captions as authoritative claims.
- Old tables embedded in manuscript drafts as canonical numbers.
