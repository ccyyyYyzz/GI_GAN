# Related-Work Differentiation: "Certify What You Measure, Govern What You Cannot"

Our contribution is **epistemic/accountability**, not reconstruction quality. All six threat papers use the same range/null geometry (`P_R = A†A`, `P_0 = I − A†A`, `A P_0 = 0`) to **reconstruct**. We use it to **(a) prove a verifiability barrier, (b) provide a test-time measurement certificate, and (c) meter an honesty dial.** The table, biggest risk, unifying novelty paragraph, and threat ranking follow.

## 2. Biggest genuine pre-emption risk — and honest neutralization

**The risk is a two-part theory pincer on Pillars 1 and 2, handled by cite-as-anchor, not by claiming.**

- **(a) The barrier is not ours to claim.** Gottschling et al., *The Troublesome Kernel* (SIAM Review 2025), is the flagship-venue canonical statement that a nontrivial kernel makes null-space detail unrecoverable and consistency ≠ correctness. Selling the bare "null-space is unverifiable" as new is the fastest novelty rejection.
- **(b) The more dangerous scoop is on Pillar 2.** The flagged 2026 follow-up by the same group — **Iagaru, Gottschling, Hansen, Garnier, "On Hallucinations in Inverse Problems: Fundamental Limits and Provable Assessment Methods" (arXiv:2605.13146, May 2026)** — explicitly proposes **ground-truth-free, forward-model-only** bounds/algorithms to **assess** hallucination magnitude and faithfulness. That is the exact slot our certificate occupies.

**Neutralization (honest):**
1. Cite Gottschling 2025 as the origin anchor for the barrier; reposition Pillar 1 as the **constructive, machine-precision feasible-wrong WITNESS** (explicit `A x=y` to ~2e-15, tighter than the noisy truth), a per-instance demonstrated object rather than an existence theorem.
2. **URGENT — read arXiv:2605.13146 before submission** and write a dedicated differentiation paragraph. Our defensible delta must be the **EXACT closed-form per-mode contraction `Π_y^λ` with factor exactly `λ/(λ+σ_i²)`** (an explicit modal spectrum, RelMeasErr −3–4 orders at ~flat PSNR, uniform across BP/Tikhonov/CS-TV/learned), the **quality-vs-accountability separation**, and the **governed dial**. If 2605.13146 already has an equivalent exact per-mode operator, retreat Pillar 2's claim to the separation framing + reconstructor-agnostic plug-in protocol + dial, and say so plainly. **Do NOT rest Pillar 2 on "ground-truth-free assessment" as the novel unit — that generic slot is now occupied.**
3. Cite the acknowledged null-space-injection lineage (both Wang 2023 papers, RND-SCI, NPN, GSNR, plus older Schwab/Sønderby, Chen & Davies 2020, Schwab et al. 2019) so the mechanism's prior art is never hidden.

## 3. Novelty against the whole cluster

Every paper here uses the identical range/null geometry to **RECONSTRUCT** — install the "correct" prior in the null space for fidelity (Wang AAAI 2023; RND-SCI), learn to predict null content from `y` (NPN; GSNR), fabricate null-space-like error to benchmark detectors (HalluGen), or prove abstractly that the kernel makes such content unrecoverable (Gottschling). We use the identical geometry to **CERTIFY, BOUND, and METER**. First, we make the barrier **constructive**: an explicit feasible-wrong witness matching the same record to ~2e-15 — tighter than the noisy truth — turning "consistency ≠ correctness" from a theorem about maps into a per-instance object. Second, we give a ground-truth-free **test-time certificate `Π_y^λ`** that contracts each measured mode by **exactly `λ/(λ+σ_i²)`** — a closed-form modal spectrum, not a soft penalty or design-time diagnostic — a plug-in audit over ANY reconstructor (BP/Tikhonov/CS-TV/learned) that **separates image quality from measurement accountability** (PSNR ~flat while RelMeasErr drops 3–4 orders). Third, we convert null-space injection from all-or-nothing fill into a single-scalar, pre-registered **honesty dial** fusing VQAE structure + VQGAN detail, with `A x̂_B=y` EXACT for every `B` behind a locked 8/8 gate (LPIPS −32.6% at bounded PSNR cost). **Airtight hooks:** the ~2e-15 feasible-wrong construction; the EXACT `λ/(λ+σ_i²)` per-mode operator; the ground-truth-free, reconstructor-agnostic certificate decoupling quality from accountability; and the exact-consistency (`A x̂_B=y ∀B`) metered dual-prior dial with a pre-registered locked gate. We explicitly do **not** claim SOTA, do not beat diffusion, and never claim the measurement certifies invented texture — the point is that it provably cannot. That inversion — **reconstruct → certify/bound/meter** — is the unoccupied slot.

## 4. Threat ranking (most → least severe)

1. **Gottschling et al. 2025 (SIAM Review)** — canonical anchor for Pillar 1; amplified by the 2026 follow-up (arXiv:2605.13146) that targets Pillar 2's ground-truth-free-assessment slot. **Highest risk; read and differentiate 2605.13146 before submission.**
2. **GSNR (Gualdron-Hurtado et al. 2026)** — strongest Pillar-3 collision; confines learned content to Null(H) *and* explicitly invokes "invisible to H" + hallucination + predictability, giving the cleanest "special case" sentence.
3. **NPN (Jacome et al. 2025)** — shares scalar-metered null-confined injection and complement vocabulary; low-but-nonzero Pillar-3 risk; differentiated by soft fidelity, supervision, learned-to-be-correct intent.
4. **RND-SCI (Wang et al. 2023)** — establishes exact-consistency-by-null-injection as Pillar-3 prior art; must cite; no touch on Pillars 1–2.
5. **Wang et al. AAAI 2023 (GAN-prior / PD)** — origin of GAN+null+exact-consistency for SR; pre-empts only the bare construction; philosophically opposite.
6. **HalluGen (Kim et al. 2025)** — framing/vocabulary risk only; no exact geometry, no certificate, no impossibility proof, soft consistency; borrowed taxonomy. Lowest genuine technical threat.
