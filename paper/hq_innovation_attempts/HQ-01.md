# HQ-01 — NDS-Diff: Null-Space-Confined Diffusion over the Audited Anchor

**Status:** proposed (paper-ready method subsection + proposition + experiment/ablation design; not yet run)

**One-line thesis.** Replace the *single, statically generated* two-point detail residual
`d_A + B(d_G − d_A)` with a short reverse-diffusion trajectory whose every `x0|t` estimate is
rectified back onto the *exact* measurement null space, so a full generative posterior — not a chord
between two frozen images — is the source of null-space detail, while `A x_hat = y` holds at every
step.

This document is written to be dropped into the "Methods" section of the HQ-GI reconstruction paper.
It is grounded exclusively in the reading cards cited inline (R026 DDNM, R068 Score-SDE, R070 DDRM,
R071 DPS, R074 MCG, R075 DiffPIR) and in the exact code assets (`src/projections.py`,
`vqgan_detail_fusion.py`). **No numbers are invented**: every performance statement is either a card
result attributed to its source paper, or a pre-registered success criterion for an experiment not
yet run.

---

## 1. Motivation and precise gap

Our locked fusion writes null-space content as a *deterministic, two-point interpolation*

```
x_hat_B = x0 + P0( d_A + B (d_G − d_A) ),   A x_hat_B = y  (to ~2e-15, since A P0 = 0)
```

where `x0` is the measurement-audited LMMSE anchor, `d_A = P0(x_A − x0)` is the VQAE structure
residual, `d_G = P0(x_G − x0)` is the VQGAN detail residual (both already null-projected in
`prep_residuals`), and `B ∈ [0,1]` is the single governed scalar. The reachable set of this fusion
is a **1-D chord** (plus the radial-band generalization) inside `Null(A)`: it can only produce affine
combinations of two frozen residual images. Whatever detail neither `x_A` nor `x_G` contains is
unreachable at any `B`.

The diffusion-inverse literature closes exactly this gap by replacing the two-point blend with a
generative *posterior* over the null space:

- **DDNM (R026)** fixes the range content to `A† y` and lets a pretrained diffusion model synthesize
  *only* the null content `(I − A†A) x̄`, rectifying at every reverse step so `A x̂0|t = y` holds
  exactly for any linear degradation, with no task-specific training.
- **MCG (R074)** is the technical origin of the *null-space-only* diffusion update: for inpainting it
  literally sets the consistency operator to `A = I − PᵀP` (the orthogonal-complement / null-space
  projector), pinning measured coordinates to `y` while the orthogonal (null) components evolve from
  the diffusion prior.
- **Score-SDE (R068)** provides the conditional reverse-SDE / inpainting-as-"keep the known subset,
  synthesize the rest" paradigm that these specialize.

The gap NDS-Diff fills: **all of these use an operator-specific pseudo-inverse `A†` or an SVD**
(DDRM, R070) to define the projection. GI at 2/5/10 % uses structured DCT+Hadamard+random operators
for which we already own a *matrix-free, exact* range/null projector `ExactRangeNullProjector`
(`A P0 = 0` verified to ~2e-15). NDS-Diff is DDNM's rectification **specialized to our projector**:
it swaps `A†` for `data_anchor_flat` and `(I − A†A)` for `null_project_flat`, so the diffusion posterior
writes detail directly into `Null(A)` with hard consistency, on our operators, with **nothing trained**
(the frozen VQGAN decoder is the `x0` predictor).

Positioning (honesty about what we do *not* claim). DDNM/DPS/DiffPIR are restoration engines chasing
SOTA perception (R026, R071, R075); we do not claim to beat them. NDS-Diff imports their null-space
sampling mechanism as a *drop-in replacement for the fusion's detail source*, under our exact-consistency
invariant and the same locked val→dev multi-seed gate that governs the vqae/vqgan/fusion arms.

## 2. Method

### 2.1 Notation (all in flattened, `float64`, consistent with `prep_residuals`/`fuse`)

- `x0f = measurement.flatten_img(x0)` — the audited LMMSE anchor (flattened).
- `y` — the measurement, `y = A x_true` (noiseless clean-`y` regime).
- `P0(·) = projector.null_project_flat(·)` and `x_anchor(y) = projector.data_anchor_flat(y) = Aᵀ(AAᵀ)⁻¹y`.
- `audit(·) = projector.audit_flat(·, y)` — one-shot float-drift rectification onto `{v : Av = y}`.
- `G(z) = prior.model.decode_embeddings(z)` — the **frozen VQGAN decoder** (the `x0` predictor / RED denoiser).
- A discrete DDIM-style reverse schedule `t = T, …, 1` with `T = 25` steps; `ᾱ_t` the standard
  cumulative signal coefficient. We use the deterministic (DDIM) update by default; the stochastic
  (DDPM) variant is an ablation.

Key identity (the invariant, from DDNM R026 specialized to our projector). For **any** vector `u`,
```
x̂ = x_anchor(y) + P0(u − x_anchor(y))      ⟹      A x̂ = y   exactly,
```
because `A x_anchor(y) = y` and `A P0(·) = 0`. Equivalently, in anchor-plus-null form,
`x̂ = x0f + P0(u − x0f)` also satisfies `A x̂ = y` whenever `A x0f = y` (the anchor is data-consistent).
NDS-Diff enforces this at **every** reverse step, so consistency is never a loss term.

### 2.2 The NDS-Diff sampler

Initialize the trajectory from the frozen VQGAN detail image (the current fusion's `B=1` endpoint),
`x_T ← x_G`, encoded to a noised latent-or-pixel state per the schedule. At each reverse step `t`:

1. **Prior x0-estimate.** Obtain the diffusion model's clean-image estimate `x0|t` from the current
   state `x_t` (Tweedie posterior mean; DPS R071). With a frozen VQGAN used as the denoiser,
   `x0|t = G(quantize(encode(x_t)))` — i.e. one decode of the VQGAN's clean estimate.

2. **Exact null-space rectification (the DDNM/MCG move, our projector).**
   ```
   x0_hat_t = x0f + P0( x0|t − x0f ).
   ```
   Range content stays pinned to the audited anchor; only the null component of the prior estimate is
   admitted. This is DDNM's `x̂0|t = A†y + (I−A†A)x0|t` with `A† → data_anchor` and `(I−A†A) → P0`,
   and MCG's `A = I − PᵀP` null-only update, made exact for our matrix-free operator.

3. **(Optional) DDRM-style per-mode contraction on measured modes.** In the clean-`y` regime the
   range is fixed exactly by step 2 and this term is off. For the noisy-`y` ablation, contract the
   measured modes toward `y` with the LMMSE weight `λ_i/(λ_i + σ_i²)` (DDRM's per-singular-value
   blend `η_b`, R070; matches the certificate's `λ/(λ+σ_i²)` contraction). This is the **only** place
   NDS-Diff would relax exact consistency, and it is confined to a clearly-labelled noisy arm.

4. **Reverse update.** Form `x_{t-1}` from `x_t` and `x0_hat_t` by the DDIM/DDPM update
   (Score-SDE R068 discretization). Because `x0_hat_t` is exactly consistent, the injected diffusion
   noise `g·z` (DDPM) or the deterministic drift (DDIM) perturbs `x_t`, but the *next* step's
   rectification (step 2) re-pins the range — consistency is restored every step, exactly as DDNM
   guarantees for its rectified estimates.

5. **Terminal audit.** After the final step, run one `audit(x0_hat_1)` against `float64` drift and
   report `relmeaserr = relative_measurement_error(x_hat, y, measurement)`; target ≤ 1e-5.

Output `x_hat = audit( x0f + P0(x0_hat_1 − x0f) )`.

### 2.3 Reduction to the current fusion (why it can only match-or-beat, structurally)

If the sampler is collapsed to a single step (`T=1`) with the prior estimate frozen to
`x0|1 := x0f + (d_A + B(d_G − d_A))` and no stochasticity, then
```
x_hat = x0f + P0( x0|1 − x0f ) = x0f + P0( d_A + B(d_G − d_A) ),
```
which is **exactly** `fuse(("scalar", B), …)` (`vqgan_detail_fusion.py:290`, since `d_A, d_G` are
already null-projected, `P0(d_A + …) = d_A + P0(…)` up to the idempotent projection the code already
applies). Thus the current locked fusion is a degenerate `T=1`, deterministic, two-point special case
of NDS-Diff. NDS-Diff strictly enlarges the reachable set from a 1-D chord to the diffusion posterior
support inside `Null(A)`.

## 3. Proposition (exact consistency of the NDS-Diff trajectory)

**Proposition 1 (Per-step exact measurement consistency).**
Let `A ∈ R^{m×n}` (`m<n`, full row rank) with exact null projector `P0 = I − Aᵀ(AAᵀ)⁻¹A` and data
anchor `x_anchor(y) = Aᵀ(AAᵀ)⁻¹y`, so `A P0 = 0` and `A x_anchor(y) = y`. Let `x0f` satisfy
`A x0f = y`. Then for every reverse step `t` and *any* prior estimate `x0|t ∈ R^n`, the NDS-Diff
rectified estimate
```
x0_hat_t = x0f + P0( x0|t − x0f )
```
satisfies `A x0_hat_t = y` exactly. Consequently the terminal output `x_hat = x0_hat_1` satisfies
`A x_hat = y`, independent of `T`, of the schedule, of the injected diffusion noise, and of the VQGAN
weights.

*Proof.* `A x0_hat_t = A x0f + A P0 (x0|t − x0f) = y + 0 = y`, using `A x0f = y` and `A P0 = 0`. ∎

**Corollary (Governed monotone dial; ties HQ-01 → HQ-02).**
Scaling the admitted null increment by `B ∈ [0,1]`,
`x0_hat_t(B) = x0f + P0( B·(x0|t − x0f) )`, preserves `A x0_hat_t(B) = y` for all `B` (same proof,
linearity of `P0`). `B=0` recovers the pure anchor `x0f` (max fidelity); `B=1` recovers the full
NDS-Diff posterior detail (max perception). This is the exact hook HQ-02 (`B`-as-guidance-scale) turns
into a monotone perception–distortion frontier.

This proposition is the paper-grade guarantee NDS-Diff inherits from DDNM (R026) / MCG (R074) but that
those methods state only for their `A†`/`I−PᵀP` special cases; here it holds for the general
matrix-free GI operator, which is the novel technical content.

**Implementation sketch (new code only).** Add one function to `vqgan_detail_fusion.py`:

```python
def nds_diff(x0f, y, x_G, priors, projector, measurement, steps=25,
             stochastic=False, B=1.0, seed=0):
    """DDNM-style null-confined reverse loop over the frozen VQGAN decoder.
    Every step re-pins the range to the audited anchor via P0; returns x_hat with A x_hat = y."""
    x_t = ddim_noise(measurement.flatten_img(x_G).double(), t=steps)  # seed trajectory
    for t in reversed(range(1, steps + 1)):
        x0_t   = frozen_vqgan_x0_estimate(x_t, priors)                # decode(quantize(encode))
        x0_hat = x0f + B * projector.null_project_flat(x0_t - x0f)    # Prop. 1 rectification
        x_t    = ddim_step(x_t, x0_hat, t, stochastic=stochastic)     # Score-SDE (R068) update
    return projector.audit_flat(x0_hat, y)                            # terminal drift rectify
```

Route the output through the existing `fuse`→`audit_flat`→`score_method` path so it is scored and
gated identically to `fusion_balanced`. A `diffusion` arm name registers it in the canary/gate report.

## 5. Experiment and ablation design (pre-registered; no numbers invented)

**Substrate.** 64×64 STL10, structured operators at 2/5/10 %, seeds 0/1/2, `float64` projector, the
locked val→dev firewall (`cmd_gate`). Baselines: `vqae` (structure-only), `fusion_balanced` (the locked
`B*`, the `−32.6 %` LPIPS operating point), `fusion_quality_lite`.

**E1 — Headline: does a generative posterior beat the two-point blend?**
Add the `diffusion` arm (`T=25`, DDIM, `B=1`), score on seed 0 dev, select on the locked gate, report
seeds 0/1/2. **Success (pre-registered):** `relmeaserr ≤ 1e-5` AND gate LPIPS strictly below
`fusion_balanced` at a PSNR drop ≤ 0.5 dB on ≥ 2/3 seeds. (Both thresholds are the deck's own HQ-1
criteria; the LPIPS/PSNR *values* are outcomes to be measured, not asserted here.)

**E2 — Reduction sanity (falsifiable).** Run `T=1`, deterministic, prior frozen to the two-point
blend. **Expected:** metrics match `fuse(("scalar", B))` to numerical tolerance, confirming §2.3. If
they do not match, the sampler wiring is wrong — this is the unit test for the whole arm.

**E3 — Step-count ablation.** `T ∈ {1, 5, 10, 25}`. Trace LPIPS/PSNR/RAPSD vs `T`. Question: does
extra generative refinement monotonically help, plateau, or (via hallucinated null detail) hurt RAPSD?
`T=1` = fusion; the curve's slope is the *actual* value of "diffusion over blend".

**E4 — Guidance-scale sweep (HQ-02 hook).** `B ∈ {0, 0.25, 0.5, 0.75, 1.0}` via the Corollary's
scaled increment; verify monotone LPIPS-vs-PSNR ordering and `relmeaserr ≤ 1e-5` at every `B`; pick the
max-LPIPS point under a fixed PSNR-drop budget. Produces the certified P–D operating point.

**E5 — Deterministic vs stochastic.** DDIM (deterministic) vs DDPM (with `g·z` noise). Stochastic can
add texture but risks measurement-invisible HF energy → watch RAPSD and LPIPS jointly. Report which
gives the better LPIPS at matched PSNR.

**E6 — Consistency-figure.** Log `‖x_{t-1} − x_t‖` and `relmeaserr` per step to show the trajectory
converging while consistency stays at ~2e-15 (mirrors the RED convergence figure planned for HQ-7).

**E7 — Noisy-`y` ablation (only place consistency is relaxed).** Turn on the DDRM per-mode contraction
`λ/(λ+σ²)` (step 3) on a Gaussian-read-noise 5 % operator; compare PSNR/LPIPS vs exact-consistency
NDS-Diff forced to fit noisy `y`. This is a *separate labelled arm*, exactly analogous to HQ-14's
soft-consistency caveat; it is not part of the clean-`y` headline claim.

## 6. Honest assessment: is the expected gain large or marginal?

**Likely a genuine ceiling lift, but with real risk of being only moderate on this specific testbed —
and I will not overclaim it.**

Arguments it is **large**:
- It is the *only* HQ point that changes the **source** of null detail from a two-point chord to a full
  posterior. HQ-2/4/9/10/11 are refinements of *this* substrate (the deck itself ranks HQ-1 the #1
  ceiling bet). Enlarging a 1-D reachable set to a diffusion posterior is a categorical, not
  incremental, change.
- DDNM/DDRM/DPS/DiffPIR (R026/R070/R071/R075) each report large realism gains from null-space
  generative synthesis over deterministic baselines *in their own settings*; the mechanism transfers.

Arguments it may be **marginal / where it can disappoint**:
- **The prior is not a real diffusion model.** Our "diffusion" denoiser is the *frozen VQGAN*
  (`decode_embeddings`), used zero-training. A VQGAN autoencode-and-requantize is a much weaker
  `x0`-predictor than the pretrained DDPMs in R026/R070; the posterior it defines may be close to a
  neighborhood of `x_G` itself, in which case NDS-Diff's reachable content is not dramatically richer
  than the `d_G` the fusion already uses. This is the single biggest reason the gain could be modest,
  and it is honest to flag it up front. (HQ-3's code-transformer or a real latent diffusion prior would
  raise this ceiling; NDS-Diff's *machinery* is agnostic to which `x0`-predictor is plugged in.)
- **64×64 STL10 is small and low-frequency.** The perceptual headroom above `fusion_balanced` at
  bounded PSNR may be a few LPIPS points, not a step change — the deck's own "marginal-gain risks"
  section makes the analogous point for the interpolation methods.
- **Stochasticity can backfire.** Injected diffusion noise lives partly in `Null(A)` and is therefore
  measurement-invisible; if mis-scheduled it inflates RAPSD (perceptually wrong texture) without
  improving LPIPS — hence E5/E6 as guardrails.

**Net.** NDS-Diff is the *highest-leverage substrate change* in the deck and the correct thing to build
first, but its realized gain is gated by the strength of the frozen `x0`-predictor. Expected outcome:
a real, gate-verified LPIPS improvement over `fusion_balanced` at bounded PSNR that is **larger than any
pure re-interpolation of the same two residuals (HQ-9/HQ-10)**, but **not** a diffusion-SOTA-sized jump
until a stronger prior replaces the frozen VQGAN. The paper contribution is robust regardless: the exact
per-step consistency (Prop. 1) on general matrix-free GI operators is novel even if the empirical delta
is moderate, and E2/E3 make the claim falsifiable and honestly quantified rather than asserted.

## 7. Grounding (cards, verbatim mechanism attribution)

- **R026 DDNM/DDNM+** — range fixed to `A†y`, null synthesized as `(I−A†A)x̄`, per-step rectification
  `x̂0|t = A†y + (I−A†A)x0|t` giving exact `A x̂0|t = y`; DDNM+ range-scaling `Σ_t A†(Ax0|t − y)` under
  noise. NDS-Diff = this with `A† → data_anchor_flat`, `(I−A†A) → null_project_flat`.
- **R074 MCG** — technical origin of null-space-only diffusion update; inpainting sets `A = I − PᵀP`
  (our `P0`), pinning measured coords to `y`, evolving the orthogonal (null) components from the prior.
- **R070 DDRM** — per-singular-value case split (`s_i=0` ⇒ null/prior-only; `s_i>0` ⇒ measured with
  blend `η_b`); source of the optional per-mode `λ/(λ+σ²)` contraction in step 3 (noisy arm).
- **R068 Score-SDE** — conditional reverse-SDE / inpainting-as-"keep known subset, synthesize rest";
  the DDIM/DDPM discretization used for the reverse update (step 4).
- **R071 DPS** — Tweedie posterior-mean `x̂0 = E[x0|x_t]` as the per-step clean estimate; soft-gradient
  guidance is the noisy-regime generalization we *replace* with hard projection in the clean regime.
- **R075 DiffPIR** — HQS split with a proximal data step; comparator establishing that soft/proximal
  consistency (unlike ours) does not keep `A x_hat = y` exact — sharpens our exact-consistency claim.
