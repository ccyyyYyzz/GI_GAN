# IP-09 — Re-axing the Perception–Distortion Plane: Measurement Accountability as an Orthogonal Third Coordinate

*Artifact for "Certify What You Measure, Govern What You Cannot." Strengthens Pillar 3 (the governed dial) and the quality/accountability Separation. Every number below is drawn from persisted repository data; every citation is grounded in `GI99_reading_cards.json`. Honesty red line held: the third axis measures **fidelity to the record `y`**, never the correctness of invented null-space content.*

---

## 1. The reframing

The imaging-reconstruction community evaluates on a two-dimensional plane whose axes are **distortion** `D` (full-reference error against a ground truth, e.g. RMSE/PSNR) and **perception** `P` (a no-reference or distributional realism index, e.g. NIQE/PI, or LPIPS used as a perceptual proxy). Blau & Michaeli proved this plane has a hard, unattainable region: for any non-invertible degradation and any full-reference distortion measure, lowering `D` provably forces the reconstruction distribution away from natural-image statistics, raising `P` — a monotone, convex perception–distortion bound no algorithm can cross (R061). Their rate–distortion–perception extension (R062) shows the same tension re-priced in bits, and gives the cautionary MNIST demonstration in which high-perception reconstructions look natural yet carry the **wrong digit identity** — perceptual quality is not accuracy. The PIRM challenge (R065) operationalized the plane as a benchmark and confirmed even a human-validated perceptual index tracks opinion only coarsely (Spearman 0.83, unreliable within-region). LPIPS (R063) is the standard perceptual metric, but it is purely a **quality** metric: a deep-feature distance calibrated to human 2AFC judgements, with no notion of what the measurement operator can or cannot vouch for.

**The gap.** Both `D` and `P` are ground-truth-dependent or dataset-statistical, and both are silent on the one question an honest imaging report must answer: *of what I am showing you, how much is backed by the measurement `y`, and how much did I invent?* The plane has no coordinate for **measurement accountability**.

**The re-axing.** We add a third, ground-truth-free axis

> `V` = measurement infidelity = `RelMeasErr` = ‖A x̂ − y‖ / ‖y‖ (equivalently, its complement, the range-share ρ of the reconstruction's energy that the operator explains).

`V` is not a quality metric and not a distortion metric. It is an **accountability** coordinate: it reads out, per reconstruction, the residual of the very record the instrument produced. `V ≈ 0` certifies "everything the measurement constrains is satisfied"; it says **nothing** about the null-space content, which is exactly why a separate governed dial (not the certificate) is responsible for that content.

**The claim of this innovation point.** On the *measurement-safe manifold* — the set of reconstructions produced by injecting content only into the null space of `A` — the accountability axis `V` is **provably orthogonal** to the entire perception–distortion plane. Moving the governed dial `B` sweeps a reconstruction across the full `(D, P)` plane (trading distortion for perception exactly as Blau–Michaeli predict) while leaving `V` pinned at the numerical floor. The perception–distortion tradeoff is therefore not abolished — it is **lifted off the accountability axis**: our dial buys perceptual quality by moving *along* the (D,P) tradeoff, and it does so at **zero cost in measurement fidelity**, because `A P_0 = 0`. This is a decomposition the whole-image `(D,P)` formulation structurally cannot express (R061 innovation spark): their `λ`-as-Lagrange GAN dial moves the reconstruction *everywhere*, including the measured subspace, so it cannot hold `V` fixed; ESRGAN's `α` network-interpolation dial (R064) has the same defect, interpolating in weight space and perturbing measured modes. Our scalar `B` leaves every measured mode invariant by construction.

## 2. Proposition (orthogonality of the accountability axis on the measurement-safe manifold)

**Setup.** Let `A ∈ R^{m×n}` be the forward operator with pseudo-inverse `A†`, range projector `P_R = A†A`, and null projector `P_0 = I − A†A`, so `A P_0 = 0`. Let `x̂_0` be any fixed base reconstruction (in our instantiation, the VQAE refiner output `W=0`). Define the **measurement-safe manifold** as the one-parameter family produced by null-space-confined injection,

> `x̂_B = x̂_0 + P_0( d_A + B (d_G − d_A) )`,  `B ∈ [0,1]`,

where `d_A, d_G` are the (null-projected) VQAE-structure and VQGAN-detail residuals and `B` is the governed scalar dial. (This is exactly the fusion identity used in the repository: `x_hat = x0 + P0( d_A + W (d_G − d_A) )`.)

Let the three plane coordinates be `D(x̂) = Δ(x̂, x*)` for any full-reference distortion measure `Δ` and ground truth `x*`; `P(x̂) = perceptual index / LPIPS(x̂)`; and the accountability coordinate `V(x̂) = ‖A x̂ − y‖ / ‖y‖`.

**Proposition.** For every `B ∈ [0,1]`,

1. **`V` is constant along the manifold and equal to the base residual:** `A x̂_B = A x̂_0`, hence `V(x̂_B) = V(x̂_0)`, independent of `B`. In particular `dV/dB = 0` exactly.
2. **`D` and `P` are, in general, non-constant along the manifold:** because `Δ` and the perceptual index depend on the null component, and `x̂_B − x̂_0 = P_0(d_A + B(d_G − d_A))` lies in a non-trivial `Null(A)` (for GI, `m ≪ n`), generic `B` changes both `D` and `P`.

Therefore the map `B ↦ (D, P, V)` traces a curve that lives entirely in the plane `{V = V(x̂_0)}` yet moves within `(D, P)`. The accountability axis is orthogonal to the perception–distortion plane *on this manifold*: no `(D,P)` motion produced by the dial has any `V` component.

**Proof sketch.**
- *(1)* `A x̂_B = A x̂_0 + A P_0(d_A + B(d_G − d_A))`. Since `A P_0 = 0`, the second term is the zero vector for **every** coefficient, so `A x̂_B = A x̂_0` identically in `B`. The residual `‖A x̂_B − y‖` — and hence `V` — is thus independent of `B`; differentiating, `dV/dB = 0`. This is the same geometric fact the null-space-reconstruction literature uses to *guarantee consistency* (Wang AAAI 2023 fix-range/fill-null `A x̂ ≡ y`, R001; RND-SCI `Φx̃ = y`); we use it instead to guarantee that **the accountability coordinate does not move when we move the dial** — same geometry, inverted purpose.
- *(2)* `x̂_B − x̂_0 = P_0 u(B)` with `u(B) = d_A + B(d_G − d_A)`. For it to leave `D` and `P` unchanged one would need `P_0 u(B)` to be `Δ`-invisible and perception-invisible, which holds only in degenerate cases (`d_A = d_G`, or a trivial null space). In GI, `dim Null(A) = n − rank(A) > 0`, and the VQAE/VQGAN detail residuals differ, so the null injection is visible to both a full-reference `Δ` and to LPIPS. Empirically (§3) `D` and `P` both move monotonically by tens of percent across the same sweep on which `V` is flat. ∎

**Scope / honesty caveat (load-bearing).** Orthogonality is a statement about **measurement fidelity**, not correctness. The Proposition does **not** say the injected null content is correct — R062's wrong-digit result and our own P1 converse (a feasible-wrong twin matching `y` to ~2e-15) prove it can be arbitrarily wrong while `V` stays at the floor. That is precisely the point of a *third* axis: `V = 0` is necessary honesty (you matched the record) but not sufficient truth (the null part is invented and is labelled so by the dial). The certificate audits the measured ledger; the `B`-axis governs — and admits as invented — the null ledger.

**Figure title (suggested).** *The accountability axis: null-space governance moves perception and distortion while measurement fidelity stays pinned.*

### 3.1 Axes

| axis | symbol | quantity | column / source | direction |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| x | `D` | distortion = centered RMSE vs ground truth (report PSNR on a secondary tick) | `centered_rmse` (per-image), or `psnr` | lower-left = lower distortion |
| y | `P` | perception = LPIPS (R063), lower = more perceptually natural | `lpips` (per-image) | lower = better perception |

## 4. Positioning: why this is a contribution, not a re-plot

1. **Extends R061/R062 rather than competing.** We keep their tradeoff intact (Series 3, panel a) and add the coordinate their whole-image formulation cannot represent. Their `λ`/`α` dials cannot hold `V` fixed; ours does by construction (`A P_0 = 0`). The re-axing turns their impossibility result into a **decomposition**: a certified-measured ledger (fixed `V`) ⊕ a governed-null ledger (dialable `D,P`).
2. **Neutralizes the strongest reviewer instinct.** "Better LPIPS = better recovery" is exactly the confusion R063's LPIPS and R065's coarse perceptual index invite. Panel (c) — perception sweeping while accountability is pinned — is the visual refutation: perceptual movement carries **zero** measurement-fidelity information. This is the geometric companion to IP-10's LPIPS-up/certificate-flat figure, promoting it from a single scalar to a whole plane.
3. **Differentiates from the #1 threat on the governance axis.** Iagaru et al. (arXiv:2605.13146) *assess* hallucination with forward-model-only bounds but have **no dial** — no way to *move* a reconstruction across the perception–distortion plane while metering fidelity. The `(D,P,V)` trajectory is a picture only a **constructive governor** can draw; assessment-only work can plot a point, not a controlled path. (See `NOVELTY_THREAT_DIFFERENTIATION.md`: assessment vs governance is the clean axis.)
4. **Honesty red line, visibly enforced.** The figure's own geometry states the limit: `V ≈ 0` is a horizontal sheet (you matched the record) that says nothing about whether the null content on that sheet is true. The dial axis is labelled *invented detail*; the accountability axis certifies only the measured ledger. Nothing in the plot claims the measurement certifies the injected texture.
