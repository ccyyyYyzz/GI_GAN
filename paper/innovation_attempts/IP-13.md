## 0. One-line contribution

Our governed dial already carries **two** null-space contributions — an injected *structure* residual from the VQAE prior and an injected *detail* residual from the VQGAN prior — collapsed into a single interpolation scalar. We split that scalar into **two independently governed knobs**, `a` (structure) and `b` (detail), and issue a **separate per-knob null-energy receipt** for each, so a user meters "how much structure vs. how much invented texture" was added, with each contribution *provably confined to the measurement null space* (`A P_0 = 0`) and therefore measurement-safe at every setting.

---

## 1. Motivation and prior-art grounding

VQ-VAE-2 (card 44) factorizes an image into a **top** code that carries global *structure/geometry* and a **bottom** code (conditioned on top) that carries local *detail/texture*; the two levels "encode complementary information," and the paper is explicitly motivated by discarding >80% of the data as perceptually recoverable detail. Card 44's own `innovation_spark` states the extension we realize here verbatim:

> "Their explicit factorization of structure (top) vs detail (bottom) suggests our null-space dial could itself be TWO knobs — one governing injected structure, one governing injected detail — letting a certificate report separately how much of each was invented."

Our fusion instantiates the same coarse/fine split with two *different priors* rather than two levels of one: the **VQAE** (card 43, the discrete-codebook autoencoder whose L2 reconstructions are "systematically blurry," i.e. structure-preserving but detail-poor) supplies the structure residual, and the **VQGAN** (card 56, whose perceptual + adversarial codebook is an unconstrained *detail* generator) supplies the detail residual. Card 56's spark notes that VQGAN's adaptive `λ` balances fidelity against realism in an *entangled* way at the decoder's last layer; our contribution is to make that balance **geometrically exact and separately auditable** by routing each contribution through `P_0` under its own knob.

Positioning against the closest interfaces:
- **CodeFormer (card 59)** exposes a *single* scalar `w ∈ [0,1]` whose Controllable Feature Transformation trades fidelity for quality on **entangled** features — so quality genuinely costs measurement fidelity. Our knobs are (i) two, not one, and (ii) each costs *nothing* on the measurement (`A x̂ = y` exact ∀(a,b)).
- **ViT-VQGAN (card 57)** factorizes *where a code lives* from *what it decodes to*; card 57's spark reads this as a subspace decoupling that "mirrors our range/null split." The two-knob form makes that mirror literal: structure and detail codes are decoded, projected into the same null subspace, and metered independently there.
- **Deep Decoder (card 42)** controls a reconstructor's *reachable set* architecturally (capacity `k`); our two knobs control null-space *content* post hoc — complementary knobs on the same reachable-set idea, but ours are auditable at test time with exact residual.

The single-pixel / ghost-imaging setting (card 14) is where this matters: in the `M < N` undersampled regime the community routinely tunes reconstructions to `χ²/M ≈ 1` (measurement consistency), which says nothing about the null space — precisely the content our two receipts quantify and label invented.

## 2. Formulation

### 2.1 Current single-knob dial (what exists)

Let `A ∈ R^{m×n}` be the fixed forward operator, `y = A x_true` the record, `x_0 = A† y`-type LMMSE anchor, and `P_0 = I − A†A` the null-space projector (`A P_0 = 0`). From the two priors we form the **null-projected residuals** (exactly as in `vqgan_detail_fusion.py`, `prep_residuals`):

```
d_A = P_0 (x_A − x_0)      # VQAE structure residual, lives in Null(A)
d_G = P_0 (x_G − x_0)      # VQGAN detail residual,    lives in Null(A)
```

The shipped `fuse()` combines them with one scalar `W` as an **interpolation**:

```
x̂_W = x_0 + P_0( d_A + W (d_G − d_A) )                       (current, locked)
     = x_0 + P_0( (1−W) d_A + W d_G )
```

so structure and detail are yoked: turning detail up (`W→1`) necessarily turns structure down. Both endpoints and every interior `W` satisfy `A x̂_W = y` to ~2e-15 (the locked 8/8 gate).

### 2.2 Proposed two-knob dial (this contribution)

Free the two coefficients:

```
    ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
    │   x̂_{a,b} = x_0 + P_0( a · d_A + b · d_G ),   a,b ≥ 0    │   (IP-13)
    └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

`a` is the **structure knob**, `b` is the **detail knob**. The current dial is exactly the diagonal slice `a = 1−W, b = W`; the two-knob form is a strict superset (the full non-negative quadrant, including `a=b=0` = bare anchor, `(1,0)` = structure-only, `(0,1)` = detail-only).

**Proposition (exact measurement-safety, ∀ a,b).**
For every `(a,b)`, `A x̂_{a,b} = y`.

*Proof sketch.* `d_A, d_G ∈ Null(A)` by construction (each is `P_0(·)`), and `Null(A)` is a linear subspace, so `a d_A + b d_G ∈ Null(A)` and `P_0(a d_A + b d_G) = a d_A + b d_G`. Hence `A x̂_{a,b} = A x_0 + A(a d_A + b d_G) = A x_0 = y` (the anchor is measurement-consistent, `A A† y = y` on the range). The residual `‖A x̂_{a,b} − y‖` is bounded by floating-point round-off in `P_0` — empirically ~2e-15, the same gate the single-knob dial already passes, independent of `a,b`. ∎

This is the honesty red line stated as a theorem: **no choice of `(a,b)` can move the measured (range) coordinates**, so no setting can be dishonestly presented as "recovering more of the object." Both knobs live entirely in the unverifiable ledger.

### 2.3 Per-knob null-energy receipts

Because `d_A` and `d_G` are generally **not orthogonal** in `Null(A)`, "how much structure vs. detail" needs a decomposition that (i) sums to the total injected null energy and (ii) attributes cross-energy honestly. Define total injected null content `u = a d_A + b d_G` and report a three-line receipt:

```
E_struct(a,b)  =  a² ‖d_A‖²                         # pure structure energy
E_detail(a,b)  =  b² ‖d_G‖²                         # pure detail energy
E_cross(a,b)   =  2 a b ⟨d_A, d_G⟩                  # shared / correlated energy
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
E_null(a,b)    =  ‖u‖²  =  E_struct + E_detail + E_cross   (identity, exact)
```

and the honest **invented-fraction receipt** relative to the anchor's own energy:

```
r_struct = E_struct / ‖x_0‖² ,   r_detail = E_detail / ‖x_0‖² ,   r_null = ‖u‖² / ‖x_0‖²
```

Reporting `E_cross` explicitly is the honest move: it prevents double-counting and makes visible *how independent* the two priors' inventions actually are (a large `|E_cross|` warns the user that the "structure" and "detail" knobs are not cleanly separable for that image — an auditable caveat, not a hidden one). A cosine `⟨d_A,d_G⟩/(‖d_A‖‖d_G‖)` near 0 certifies the two knobs are close to orthogonal contributions; near 1 flags redundancy.

**Every receipt quantity is an amount of *invented* null content, never a certificate of correctness.** They answer "how much did I add, and of what kind," not "is what I added true."

### 2.4 Optional per-band refinement (already supported by the code)

`vqgan_detail_fusion.py` already carries a `("band", ...)` mode (`weight_map` / `apply_band`) that applies a frequency-band weight vector instead of a scalar. IP-13 subsumes this cleanly: replace scalars `(a,b)` by band-diagonal operators `(A_diag, B_diag)` acting on the null residuals per frequency band, and emit the receipt **per band**. This gives a structure/detail ledger resolved over spatial frequency at no extra measurement cost — a natural "moonshot" extension, but not required for the core claim.

## 3. Ablation design (buildable from existing locked assets, zero retraining)

All assets exist: frozen VQAE/VQGAN priors + refiners per seed, the locked hash-clean STL10 split (`LOCKED_N = 512`, disjoint from every consumed set), the projector with exact `null_project_flat` / `audit_flat`, seeds `{0,1,2}`, and the `LPIPS/PSNR/RMSE/RAPSD/RelMeasErr` scorer. The only code change is generalizing `fuse()` from `("scalar", W)` to `("ab", a, b)`:

```python
# drop-in generalization of vqgan_detail_fusion.fuse (kind == "ab")
u   = a * d_A + b * d_G                       # both already = P0(...)
d_F = projector.null_project_flat(u)          # idempotent re-projection (round-off guard)
xhat = projector.audit_flat(x0f + d_F, y)     # same exact audit as the locked dial
# receipts:
E_struct = (a*a) * (d_A @ d_A)
E_detail = (b*b) * (d_G @ d_G)
E_cross  = 2*a*b * (d_A @ d_G)
```

**Design.** One-shot, pre-registered like the locked gate; report per-seed and seed-averaged with the existing image bootstrap.

| Arm | (a, b) | Purpose |
|---|---|---|
| Anchor | (0, 0) | `x_0` only; floor for LPIPS/PSNR and receipts (`E=0`). |
| Structure-only | (1, 0) | VQAE structure injected, no detail. |
| Detail-only | (0, 1) | VQGAN detail injected, no structure. |
| Locked diagonal | (1−W\*, W\*) | The shipped balanced/quality-lite operating point (reproduces the current result exactly — sanity anchor). |
| Structure sweep | a ∈ {0,.25,.5,.75,1}, b = b\* | Isolate the structure knob at fixed detail. |
| Detail sweep | a = a\*, b ∈ {0,.25,.5,.75,1} | Isolate the detail knob at fixed structure — the IP-10-style "LPIPS moves, certificate flat" curve, now attributed to *detail alone*. |
| Grid | (a,b) ∈ {0,.5,1}² | 2-D dial surface for the headline figure. |

**Primary outcomes (per arm, per seed, seed-averaged + image-bootstrapped):**
1. `RelMeasErr` — must stay ≤ 1e-5 (target ~2e-15) for **every** `(a,b)` [the Proposition, verified empirically; this is the measurement-safety gate].
2. `E_struct`, `E_detail`, `E_cross`, `r_struct`, `r_detail`, cosine(`d_A,d_G`) — the receipts.
3. `LPIPS`, `PSNR`, `RMSE`, `RAPSD` — perceptual/distortion, to show the detail knob buys perception (LPIPS↓) while the structure knob buys distortion (RMSE↓/PSNR↑), a clean *functional separation* of the two knobs.

**Falsifiable success conditions (pre-registered):**
- **S1 (safety):** `RelMeasErr ≤ 1e-5` on all arms, all seeds → the two-knob generalization does not break exact consistency. *(Guaranteed by the Proposition; the ablation is a confirmatory numerical check.)*
- **S2 (separability):** the detail sweep moves LPIPS with |ΔPSNR| smaller than the structure sweep's, and the structure sweep moves PSNR with |ΔLPIPS| smaller than the detail sweep's, in ≥ 2/3 seeds → the two knobs are functionally distinct, not redundant.
- **S3 (additive receipt):** `E_struct + E_detail + E_cross = ‖u‖²` to round-off on every image → the ledger is exact and complete.
- **S4 (honest cross-term):** report mean cosine(`d_A,d_G`); if |cosine| is large, state plainly that the knobs are correlated for this operator/dataset (an auditable limitation, not a failure).

**Negative / null results are publishable and honest.** If S2 fails (knobs redundant, cosine≈1), the finding is "for this operator the two priors invent nearly the same null content — one knob suffices," which still strengthens the honesty framing (we *measured and reported* the redundancy). Nothing about correctness of the invented content is ever asserted either way.

## 5. Honesty statement (red line, explicit)

The two receipts report the **magnitude and type of invented null-space content**. They certify only that the injection is confined to `Null(A)` (`A x̂_{a,b} = y` exactly) — i.e. that it is *measurement-safe*, not that it is *correct*. No structure or detail quantity here is a claim that the invented content matches the true object; by the paper's own converse (P1), the null component is provably unverifiable. The contribution of IP-13 is a **finer honesty ledger** — two metered knobs each with its own invented-energy receipt — not a stronger recovery claim.
