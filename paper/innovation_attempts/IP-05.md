# IP-05 — The Inversion Catalogue

*Realization of Innovation Point 5 ("Same geometry, opposite epistemic stance") as a paper-ready Related-Work subsection: table + prose + proposition, grounded entirely in `GI99_reading_cards.json`.*

**Strengthens.** P4 (Stance) + Related-Work (novelty defense).
**Honesty red line (held throughout).** Every row below shows a paper *reconstructing* with the range/null geometry and our *inverted* use of the same geometry to certify, bound, or meter. The right-hand column never claims the measurement certifies invented content — it claims the opposite: that the invented (null) content is provably outside what the measurement can certify. Our dial supplies null content that is *labelled invented by construction*; the certificate audits only the *measured (range)* ledger.

## 1. The subsection prose (drop-in)

Every range/null result in our corpus is built on one construction. Write the reconstruction as

> **x̂ = A†y + (I − A†A) q = A†y + P₀ q,**

fixing the range component to the pseudo-inverse A†y and filling the null component P₀q. This single line is the load-bearing object of the field. Wang et al. (AAAI 2023) fix the range and let a GAN prior fill P₀q, reporting measurement consistency to ~145 dB by construction [card 0]. Wang et al. (2023, RND-SCI) do the same for coded-aperture spectral imaging at 91 fps, computing the range term in closed form and hallucinating only the null term [card 3]. NPN (Jacome et al., NeurIPS 2025) learns a projection of the null content from y, opening with the observation that "any component in the null-space may be arbitrarily modified by the denoiser" [card 4]. GSNR (Gualdron-Hurtado et al., 2026) restricts a graph-smooth prior to Null(H) via the null-restricted Laplacian T = PₙLPₙ [card 2]. DDNM (Wang et al., 2023) lets a pretrained diffusion model synthesize the null content while pinning A x̂ = y exactly [card 19]. Deep Null-Space Learning (Schwab et al.) builds L = Id + (Id − A⁺A)N so that ALx = Ax by construction [card 14]; Deep Decomposition Learning splits the estimate into range and null via P_R = H⁺H, P₀ = I − H⁺H and learns both from ground-truth pairs [card 15]; IDBP (Tirer & Giryes, 2018) drives all restoration dynamics through Q_H = I − H†H while backward-projecting onto {Hx = y} [card 34].

**Every one of these installs content in P₀ in order to RECONSTRUCT — to raise fidelity, PSNR, realism, or speed — and treats measurement consistency as a correctness proxy.** Our contribution occupies the one slot none of them fill: we use the *identical* x̂ = A†y + P₀q to **certify what the measurement accounts for, bound what it provably cannot, and meter the invented remainder**. The unoccupied position is not a new operator; it is the inverted purpose — reconstruct → certify / bound / meter.

The inversion is not our imposition on the field; its proponents state it against themselves. NPN's premise that "any null-space component may be arbitrarily modified" [card 4] *is* our converse: if any null content is admissible under the same y, no measurement can single out the true one. Bhadra et al.'s tomographic-hallucination analysis states plainly that null-space hallucinations "cannot be assessed without the ground-truth object" [card 21] — again our converse, written by a reconstruction paper. Iagaru et al. (2026), the closest concurrent ground-truth-free-limits work, prove that a consistent decoder can only hallucinate detail almost invisible to the measurement (‖f(x+x_det) − f(x)‖ ≤ 2ε) [card 85] — the same barrier. We cite these as anchors for the barrier and claim only what they do not deliver: an **exact per-mode certificate** (contraction λ/(λ+σᵢ²), not a diameter/kernel-size bound or a qualitative measurement-space map) and a **governed, exact-consistency injection dial** (A x̂_B = y for every B, behind a pre-registered gate) that no assessment-only paper contributes.

## 3. Proposition (the catalogue as one formal statement)

**Proposition (the occupied vs. unoccupied slot).**
Let A be a linear forward operator with nontrivial null space, P₀ = I − A†A ≠ 0, and let y = Ax be a noiseless record. For any reconstruction of the form x̂ = A†y + P₀q with q chosen by a prior/network:

1. **(Consistency is free.)** A x̂ = A A†y + A P₀q = y + 0 = y, for *every* q, since A P₀ = 0. Hence measurement consistency is invariant to the null content [cards 0, 3, 14, 15, 19, 34].
2. **(Converse / unverifiability.)** For any q′ ≠ q, the twin x̂′ = A†y + P₀q′ satisfies A x̂′ = y identically. No functional of y alone can distinguish x̂ from x̂′. Therefore the null content is not certifiable from the measurement [cards 4, 21, 85].
3. **(Inversion.)** Rows 1–10 above all *choose* q to reconstruct (maximize fidelity/PSNR/realism/speed). We instead (i) **certify** the range ledger by the exact per-mode contraction λ/(λ+σᵢ²) [P2; contrast bound/diagnostic in cards 21, 85], and (ii) **meter** q by a scalar dial B with A x̂_B = y exact ∀B and a pre-registered gate [P3; cards 14, 19, 34], **labelling P₀q_B invented by construction**.

*Proof sketch.* Parts 1–2 are immediate from A P₀ = 0 and A A† = I on range(A); they are the algebraic content of the cited null-space constructions read as a converse. Part 3 is definitional: the certificate is a function of A's singular spectrum and the noise level only (no ground truth, no q), so it is well-defined and identical for any reconstructor sharing A; the dial changes only P₀q_B, leaving A x̂_B = y by Part 1. No step asserts the measurement certifies q_B — Part 2 forbids exactly that, and the dial's output is declared invented. ∎

**Reading.** The whole catalogue collapses to: *the field varies q to reconstruct; we hold the certificate on the part q cannot touch and meter q as declared invention.* The novel unit is the inversion of purpose, not a new decomposition.

## 4. One experiment-design proposal to make the catalogue empirical (optional, cheap)

*Goal:* turn the table into a single reproducible figure that the Related-Work text can point to.

- **Setup.** Fix one operator A already in the repo (GI/fastMRI). Instantiate the *same* x̂ = A†y + P₀q under three qs drawn from card families: a diffusion null-fill (DDNM-style, card 19), a learned null prior (DDN/NPN-style, cards 15/4), and our governed VQAE+VQGAN dial at setting B (card 14/19 lineage).
- **Measure two axes per q.** (a) Measurement residual ‖A x̂ − y‖ — predicted flat at ~2e-15 for all three by Proposition Part 1. (b) Per-mode certificate spectrum λ/(λ+σᵢ²) — predicted *identical* across all three (it depends on A, not q), directly visualizing "same geometry, one certificate, many reconstructions."
- **Payoff.** The figure shows every catalogue method landing on the *same* residual and the *same* certificate while their null content (and any GT error) differs arbitrarily — the empirical face of Proposition Parts 1–3, and a direct demonstration that the certificate audits the operator, not the reconstructor (ties to IP-3). No new training; reuses existing assets.
